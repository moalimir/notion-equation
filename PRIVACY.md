# Privacy Policy for Notion Equation

Effective date: July 5, 2026

Notion Equation does not collect, store, sell, or transmit personal information, browsing history, Notion content, equations, analytics, or usage data.

The extension reads and modifies the active Notion page only after the user clicks **Convert equations** or invokes the configured keyboard shortcut. Page processing happens locally in the browser. No page content is sent to the developer or any third party.

The extension does not use remote code, advertising, tracking, cookies, external APIs, or accounts. It does not retain page content after conversion finishes.

Chrome permissions are used as follows:

- `activeTab`: temporarily allows the extension to operate on the current tab after an explicit user action.
- `scripting`: injects the packaged conversion script into that active tab. The script refuses unsupported non-Notion pages.

Questions or privacy concerns can be reported through the project's support page: https://github.com/moalimir/notion-equation/issues

Notion Equation is an independent project and is not affiliated with Notion Labs, Inc.
