| --- | --- | --- | --- |

| Screenshot 2 | `assets/screenshot-02-before-after.jpg` | 1280×800 | JPEG, RGB, no alpha |

| Marquee promo tile | `assets/marquee-1400x560.jpg` | 1400×560 | JPEG, RGB, no alpha |

The extension icon intentionally retains transparent padding. Promotional JPEGs are opaque RGB images and cannot contain an alpha channel.
